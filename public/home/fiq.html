1. What services do you offer?

We provide a range of services, including listing and managing PG accommodations, offering meal plans through our mess services, and providing stationary essentials for students and working professionals.

2. How can I find a PG near me?

You can use our search feature on the homepage. Enter your location and preferences to view available PG options.

3. Are the PG accommodations verified?

Yes, all PGs listed on our site undergo a verification process to ensure safety and quality.

4. What meal plans do you offer?

We offer flexible meal plans, including vegetarian, non-vegetarian, and mixed options, tailored to meet different dietary needs.

5. How do I sign up for a meal plan?

You can sign up for a meal plan through our website. Select your preferred plan and fill out the necessary details.

6. Who do I contact for support?

For any inquiries or support, please reach out to our customer service team via the contact form on our website or call our helpline.
